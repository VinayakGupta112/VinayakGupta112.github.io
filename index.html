Computational methods for identifying RNA binding pockets, characterizing RNA structures, and studying RNA–small-molecule interactions.

⚛️
Molecular Simulations

Molecular dynamics simulations and trajectory analysis for understanding biomolecular stability, conformational dynamics, and molecular interactions.

Developing interpretable and predictive machine-learning approaches for structural biology, genomics, and biological datasets.

Geometric Deep Learning

Exploring graph neural networks and three-dimensional geometric learning methods for representing RNA and other biomolecular structures.
